📖 Champions: Unbound Rules

⚔️ Objective

Reduce your opponent's Champion Health to 0 to win.

🎴 Setup

• Select your Champion.

• Set starting Health, Armor, AP, and Attributes.

• Players complete their setup phase before damage can be dealt.

⚡ Turn Flow

1. Refresh AP to your Champion's starting AP.

2. Play cards and use abilities.

3. Attack or use Signature Techniques.

4. Unused AP may be used for reactions.

🎲 Attribute Rolls

Roll a D20 + Attribute.

Success happens when the total meets the difficulty.

⭐ Natural 20 triggers bonus effects.

💀 Natural 1 triggers failure effects.

🔥 Signature Techniques

• Pay the AP cost.

• Roll the required Attribute check.

• Apply Success, Nat 20, or Nat 1 effects.

🗡️ Basic Attacks

• Basic Attacks cost AP.

• Champions may only Basic Attack once per turn unless an effect allows more.

🛡️ Armor

Damage removes Armor before Health unless stated otherwise.

👥 Allies

• Allies can block.

• Allies attack only if Champion abilities or effects allow them.
